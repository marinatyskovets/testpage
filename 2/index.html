Astro is a personal growth platform that helps people understand themselves and others better

Our Mission

Our mission is to create a space for emotional balance, helping people reconnect with their inner world.

Through reflection and awareness, we aim to bring more clarity and harmony into everyday life.

Our journey

Astro began in 2017 with a simple idea — that happiness, success, and harmony in relationships start with inner balance.

We wanted to create something that would help people understand themselves better and reconnect with their inner world.

From the very beginning, Astro has combined elements of psychology and ancient self-reflection practices to make self-discovery more accessible.

Astrology in Astro is used as a lens for self-discovery — a way to look within and understand your unique patterns.

Our idea has resonated with many people around the world, and our team continues to grow with those who share the same mission — helping others live with more awareness, clarity, and balance every day.

Today, Astro is available on both the App Store and Google Play, as well as on this website.
