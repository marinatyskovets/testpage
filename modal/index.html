Use these tips to get the best photo for your resume
Some employers won’t consider resumes with photos. Make sure to read the application details before adding a photo.
  • Make sure to use a new color picture in JPEG, PNG, or GIF format that is smaller than 10 MB.
  • Crop your photo so it only shows your head and shoulders.
  • If you upload a new photo, it will replace the one you already uploaded.
