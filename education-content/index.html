Expert Insights
Some people still face age discrimination at work. If your degree is older than ten years, you might want to remove the date.
  • List the schools you've been to and the diplomas or degrees you earned, starting from the last one.
  • If you didn’t attend college, list high school only.
  • You don't have to list coursework, but it can help if you lack work experience.
  • Certifications and training programs should be included in a separate section.
